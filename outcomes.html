Student Outcomes

Student Outcomes

Student Outcome 1 Analyze a complex computing problem and apply principles of computing and other relevant disciplines to identify solutions.
Student Outcome 2 and 3 An ability to design, implement, and evaluate a computer-based solution to meet a given set of computing requirements in the context of the discipline.
Student Outcome 4 Communicate effectively in professional contexts.
Student Outcome 5 ABET Student Outcome 1, 2, & 6
Student Outcome 6 Function effectively as a member of a team engaged in activities appropriate to the program discipline.
Student Outcome 7 and 8 Recognize professional responsibilities and make informed judgement in computing practice based on legal and ethical principles.
Student Outcome 9 An ability to identify and analyze user needs and to take them into account in the selection, creation, integration, evaluation, and administration of computing-based systems.
